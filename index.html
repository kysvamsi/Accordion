Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time for online orders?
The delivery time for online orders typically depends on your location and the shipping method chosen. Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days, while expedited options may deliver within 1-2 business days.
Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied with it?
Yes, we have a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days of receiving it. Please check our 'Returns and Exchanges' page for detailed instructions.
How do I reset my password for the user account?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. You will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password. Follow the provided link and enter a new password to regain access to your account.
